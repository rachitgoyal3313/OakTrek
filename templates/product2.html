Finding Your Perfect Fit

Our shoes are designed to fit true to size. For the best fit:

  • If you're between sizes, we recommend sizing up
  • For wide feet, consider going up half a size
  • The shoe should feel snug but not tight around the midfoot

Measurement Guide

To measure your foot:

  1. Stand on a piece of paper
  2. Trace your foot outline
  3. Measure the length from heel to longest toe
  4. Use our size chart to find your perfect fit

Break-in Period

Allow 1-2 days of wear for the shoes to adapt to your foot shape. The knit material will naturally stretch slightly for a custom fit.

Machine Washing Instructions

  • Remove laces and insoles before washing
  • Wash on cold, gentle cycle (max 30°C/86°F)
  • Use mild detergent, avoid bleach
  • Do not tumble dry
  • Air dry away from direct heat/sunlight

Regular Maintenance

  • Brush off dry dirt with a soft brush
  • Spot clean small stains with mild soap and water
  • Allow shoes to dry completely between wears
  • Store in a cool, dry place

What to Avoid

  • Don't use harsh chemicals or bleach
  • Avoid direct heat when drying
  • Don't store in damp conditions
  • Don't machine wash insoles
